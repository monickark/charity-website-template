We can Help for Helpless

G.Edison picks up evicted and abandoned people from the streets and gives them a home and hope.

People without a voice always caught G.Edisonâ€™s attention even as a child. "I do not know why," he says,"but each time I saw people on the streets in need of food, clothes and shelter, it tore my heart."

"It requires courage and ability to understand another person's needs and help appropriately," he says, "because many a times when you try to do good, you face the most unexpected difficulties."

Edison dreams of a permanent building of his own where he can have three separate wings for accommodating more number of abandoned men, women and the mentally challenged.

Services

Now the Home has 31 residents with various ailments and problems but beams a sense of happiness. Edison is their one man army who cooks for them, bathes them, exercises and sings with them, reads out to them and trains them in small skills.

Projects and Programmes

Those who can manage wash their own clothes and help him with small errands. Edison also has the support of 40 volunteers, mostly college students and young professionals who take turns to help him with the various jobs.

Service Tie-Ups

On one of his rounds, he saw a lady at Arapalayam bus stand paralysed on one side and in want of food. "I started bringing her food but after three days she suddenly went missing, it disturbed me a lot."

Hands of Compassion @ Madurai

Hands of Compassion is committed to helping the helpless, homeless, sick, elderly, mentally ill and destitute in Madurai, India by providing healthy food, care, and opportunity to rehabilitate in order to restore human dignity.

Hands of Compassion @ Rajapalayam

In Rajapalayam Hands of Compassion Social Service team provides Food & Clothes for the needy.Hands of Compassion supports the poor and homeless. We offer kind and practical support to our homeless friends in need, guiding them back onto the road to recovery. Many of our own volunteers have faced these issues themselves and therefore understand fully the needs of our clients.
